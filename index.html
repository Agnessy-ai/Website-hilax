About Halifax Asset Management Limited

Halifax Asset Management Limited (HAM) is a Ghanaian investment management and advisory firm, licensed and regulated by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) of Ghana. We offer tailored investment solutions, financial planning, and wealth management services with an emphasis on trust, transparency, and stewardship.

Our goal is to ensure that our partners are better today than yesterday by delivering excellence and exceeding fiduciary responsibilities.
