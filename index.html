About Halifax Asset Management Limited

Halifax Asset Management Limited (HAM) is a licensed investment management and advisory firm headquartered in Ghana. We are regulated by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) of Ghana.

At HAM, we specialize in tailored investment solutions, strategic financial planning, and wealth management services that prioritize integrity, transparency, and long-term growth.

Our mission is to deliver value that ensures our clients are better today than yesterday. We are committed to excellence and consistently strive to exceed our fiduciary responsibilities.
